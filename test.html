Yummy Yicken

So named because the first time I ate it, I could not pronounce the "ch" in "chicken". This meal is hearty and comforting, and sure to give you a heart attack.

Ingredients

  • Bear Creek Dry Broccoli Cheddar Soup
  • 8 cups Low fat milk
  • 1 pound of cheddar cheese, grated

Steps

  1. Sear chicken in butter and remove from pan
  2. Pour milk into pan with chicken drippings. Add soup mix.
  3. Simmer until thickened. Add half of the cheese, let it melt before stirring. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  4. Put the chicken back in, pour more cheese on top. Cook for 10-15min or until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce is thick and creamy
  5. Serve over rice. Top with additional cheese if desired
  6. Enjoy!
