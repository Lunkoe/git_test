Pound Cake

A rich cake containing a pound, or equal weights, of each chief ingredient, typically flour, butter, and sugar.

Do you know it?

Pound cake has traditionally been made with a pound each of butter, sugar, eggs, and flour.

Ingredients:

  • Unsalted butter softened (250g)
  • Granulated sugar (240g)
  • Eggs (4)
  • Vanilla sugar (10g)
  • Salt (pinch)
  • All-purpose flour (250g)

Steps:

  1. Split white and yolk from eggs
  2. Mix Sugar and yolk
  3. Add softened butter to the mix
  4. Add the flour
  5. Beat the white and add them to the mix
  6. Bake the mix (180Â°C / 50 minutes)
